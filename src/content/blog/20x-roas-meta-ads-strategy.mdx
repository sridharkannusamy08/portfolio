# How I Generated 20X ROAS With Meta Ads (Full Strategy)

Most businesses treat Meta Ads like a vending machine — put money in, expect leads out. That's why most businesses waste money on ads.

The reality? Meta Ads is a system. And when you build that system correctly, the returns are extraordinary.

Here's the exact strategy I used to generate **₹6,00,000 in revenue from ₹30,000 in ad spend** — a 20X return on ad spend.

---

## The Context

My client, a B2B compliance consulting firm, had never run digital ads. They were relying entirely on referrals. When referrals slowed down, they came to me.

Their goal was simple: generate qualified leads from businesses that needed compliance support.

**Starting budget: ₹30,000 over 60 days.**

---

## The 4-Stage System

### Stage 1: Audience Architecture

Before touching the Ads Manager, I spent a week on audience research.

The biggest mistake I see? Businesses targeting interest audiences blindly. Instead, I built:

- **Custom Audiences** from their existing client list (email upload)
- **Lookalike Audiences** at 1%, 2%, and 5% from the custom audience
- **Intent-based interest stacks** — not just "business" but specific targeting around compliance, construction, regulatory bodies

This alone made the initial CPL 40% lower than industry benchmarks.

### Stage 2: Creative Testing Framework

I ran 12 ad creatives in the first 30 days. Not because I love creative work — because data demands it.

My testing framework:
- 3 hooks × 2 formats (image vs. video) × 2 CTAs
- ₹200/day per ad set for 5 days before cutting losers
- Winner scaling by 30% every 48 hours (not 100% jumps — that resets the algorithm)

**The winning creative?** A simple carousel showing before/after — what compliance failures cost vs. what proper compliance saves.

### Stage 3: Landing Page Conversion

Most agencies run great ads to terrible landing pages. I treat the landing page as seriously as the ad.

For this campaign, the landing page conversion rate was **11.2%** — industry average for B2B is 2–5%.

What made the difference:
- Single, specific offer ("Free 30-Minute Compliance Audit")
- Social proof above the fold (client logos + result metrics)
- No navigation — no distractions
- Mobile-first design (73% of traffic was mobile)

### Stage 4: Follow-Up Automation

A lead without a follow-up system is just a name on a spreadsheet.

I set up:
- Immediate WhatsApp confirmation message
- 3-email nurture sequence over 5 days
- Re-targeting ads for page visitors who didn't convert

This sequence converted an additional 18% of leads that initially didn't book.

## What Most Businesses Get Wrong

**1. They optimise for clicks, not revenue.**
Clicks don't pay bills. I track conversions, cost per lead, and ultimately cost per customer.

**2. They kill ads too early.**
Meta's algorithm needs at least 50 conversions per ad set before it learns. Killing ads at day 3 based on "no results" destroys the learning phase.

**3. They ignore retargeting.**
60% of B2B decisions take more than one touchpoint. If you're not re-targeting website visitors, you're leaving money on the table.
